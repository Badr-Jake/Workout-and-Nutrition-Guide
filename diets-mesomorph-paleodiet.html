Paleo Diet
Focuses on whole foods that would have been available to Paleolithic humans, such as lean meats, fish, fruits, vegetables, nuts, and seeds. Its nutrient density is ideal for mesomorphs who need to maintain lean muscle mass.
Example
  • Chicken salad with nuts and fruits
  • Beef stir-fry with a variety of vegetables
  • Baked salmon with asparagus
